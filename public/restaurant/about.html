About Us

Mission

Our goal is to provide a premier eating experience with all-natural ingredients. Our mythical menu items are hand-made to entice, stoke, and satiate your appetite.

History

Founded in 1999 by Tike Myson, Cafe Faux started out as a small cafe in Atlantis, but has grown to over 5 mythical locations. We make food that does not exist in places that do not exist!
